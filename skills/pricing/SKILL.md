# Pricing Skill

## When to Use
- Questions about costs and pricing tiers
- Enterprise pricing inquiries
- Volume discounts
- Contract terms
- Billing questions

## Response Guidelines
1. **Be transparent**: Share published pricing openly
2. **Enterprise = conversation**: Don't quote specific enterprise prices
3. **Focus on value**: Connect pricing to business outcomes
4. **Offer to connect**: For complex pricing, offer to involve sales

## Pricing Tiers

### Starter - $99/month
- Up to 10,000 API calls/month
- 2 team members
- Email support
- 99.5% uptime SLA

### Professional - $499/month
- Up to 100,000 API calls/month
- 10 team members
- Priority email + chat support
- 99.9% uptime SLA
- SSO integration

### Enterprise - Custom
- Unlimited API calls
- Unlimited team members
- Dedicated support + SLA
- 99.95% uptime SLA
- Custom integrations
- Security review support

## Common Questions

### "Can we get a discount?"
"For annual commitments, we offer 2 months free (effectively ~17% discount). For larger volumes or multi-year deals, I'd be happy to connect you with our sales team to discuss custom pricing."

### "What's the enterprise price?"
"Enterprise pricing is customized based on your specific needs - volume, support requirements, and contract terms. Let me connect you with our sales team who can put together a proposal tailored to your situation."
