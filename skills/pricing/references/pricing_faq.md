## Common Questions

### Q: Can I change plans mid-cycle?
- **Upgrade**: Immediate, prorated billing
- **Downgrade**: Effective next billing cycle

### Q: What counts as a "document"?
One document = one API call to process, regardless of pages
- 1-page PDF = 1 document
- 50-page PDF = 1 document
- Batch of 10 files = 10 documents

### Q: Do you offer non-profit/education discounts?
Yes, 25% discount for verified non-profits and educational institutions. Contact sales.

### Q: Is there a free trial of paid features?
Yes, 14-day free trial of Pro tier. No credit card required.

### Q: What payment methods do you accept?
- Credit card (all major cards)
- ACH bank transfer (Business+)
- Wire transfer (Enterprise)
- Annual invoicing available (Business+)
